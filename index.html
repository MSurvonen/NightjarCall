Nightjar in Finland

Lintuatlas breeding data is described in scale 0-4. In this nightjar data values are only 2 and 3. Breeding probability is described with volume of nightjar sound.
  • 0 = no bird sighting
  • 1 = unlikely breeding
  • 4 = confirmed breeding
